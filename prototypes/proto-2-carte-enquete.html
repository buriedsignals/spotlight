§ 01 — What it does

Five moves that turn a lead into a publishable finding.

  • Takes a lead — URL, name, topic, document — and designs a methodology you approve before anything runs.
  • Runs research cycles with an independent fact-checker after each one. Nothing advances on one source or one agent's reasoning.
  • Every finding arrives sourced and stress-tested — two independent sources per claim, disputes flagged, affected perspectives present.
  • Review page for you or your editor — challenge any claim, re-trigger the investigator on specific points.
  • Builds a living knowledge base — entities, sources, methodology stay cross-linked in your vault.
§ 02 — What it integrates with

Pluggable where it matters. Opinionated where it counts.

  • Agent runtimes — local journalism fine-tune, Claude Code, Gemini, Codex, OpenCode.
  • Web scraping, search, evidence archiving — every source captured locally with chain of custody.
  • OSINT Navigator — 1,000+ curated OSINT tools, surfaced live per task.
  • Knowledge vault ingestion (Obsidian) — investigations become permanent, searchable memory.
  • Monitoring feeds — conflict events, disaster alerts, investigative outlets.
  • Browser automation, narrative tracking, misinformation databases — plug-in integrations.
