Built by Buried Signals — an investigative media and technology studio. Investigations with AI. The tools to run your own.

01

Intake

You bring the lead. Spotlight designs the method.

A URL, a name, a topic, a document. Spotlight drafts the methodology — angles, sources, tools, checks. You approve it before anything runs. No black box, no surprise spend.

02

Cycles

Every claim passes through an independent check.

Research runs in cycles. After each one, a separate fact-checker interrogates the work. Nothing advances on one source or one agent's reasoning.

03

Findings

Sourced. Stress-tested. Ready to publish.

Every finding ships anchored to primary documents, with two independent sources per claim, disputes flagged in the open, and affected perspectives present. You hand it to an editor, not to a verifier.

04

Review

Challenge any claim. The investigator goes back to work.

A review page made for editors. Click through findings, push back on a claim, request deeper verification. Your feedback re-triggers the investigation on specific points — not the whole case from scratch.

05

Vault

Every investigation makes the next one sharper.

Entities, sources, tools, and methodology stay cross-linked in your vault. When a new lead touches something you've already profiled, Spotlight knows. The AI remembers — you don't start from zero.
