How it works

Verification baked in. Not an afterthought.

  1. Lead. URL, name, topic, document. Spotlight designs a methodology you approve before anything runs.
  2. Cycles. Research runs with an independent fact-checker after each cycle. Nothing advances on one source or one agent's reasoning.
  3. Sourcing. Primary documents anchored, two independent sources per claim, disputes flagged, affected perspectives present.
  4. Review. Click through findings, challenge claims, re-trigger the investigator on specific points.
  5. Vault. Entities, sources, tools, methodology stay cross-linked across investigations. The AI remembers past work.
