§ 01 — What it does

Five moves that turn a lead into a publishable finding.

  • Takes a lead — URL, name, topic, document — and designs a methodology you approve before anything runs.approval gate · cycle 0
  • Runs research cycles with an independent fact-checker after each one.SIFT · isolated contextNothing advances on one source or one agent's reasoning.
  • Every finding arrives sourced and stress-tested — primary documents anchored, two independent sources per claim, disputes flagged, affected perspectives present.readiness.json · 6 criteria
  • Review page for you or your editor — click through findings, challenge claims, request deeper verification. Your feedback re-triggers the investigator on specific points.gate 1 · cycle_n+1
  • Builds a living knowledge base — entities, sources, tools, and methodology stay cross-linked across investigations.obsidian · wikilinks · persisted
