Ch. 01 — What it does

Five things, in order.

01
Lead

Takes a lead — URL, name, topic, document — and designs a methodology you approve before anything runs.

No surprise research paths. The system proposes a plan; you accept, adjust, or reject.

Runs research cycles with an independent fact-checker after each one.

Nothing advances on one source or one agent's reasoning. A second agent verifies, contradicts, escalates.

Every finding arrives sourced and stress-tested.

Primary documents anchored, two independent sources per claim, disputes flagged, affected perspectives present. Ready to publish or hand to an editor.

A review page for you or your editor — click through, challenge, request deeper verification.

Your feedback re-triggers the investigator on specific points, not the whole case.

Builds a living knowledge base across investigations.

Entities, sources, tools, and methodology stay cross-linked. The AI remembers past work and applies it to new leads — "we already profiled this shell company" instead of starting from zero.
