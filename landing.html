Built by Buried Signals — an investigative media and technology studio. Investigations with AI. The tools to run your own.

What it does

  • Takes a lead — URL, name, topic, document — and designs a methodology you approve before anything runs.
  • Runs research cycles with an independent fact-checker after each one. Nothing advances on one source or one agent's reasoning.
  • Every finding arrives sourced and stress-tested — primary documents anchored, two independent sources per claim, disputes flagged, affected perspectives present. Ready to publish or hand to an editor.
  • Review page for you or your editor — click through the findings, challenge claims, request deeper verification. Your feedback re-triggers the investigator on specific points.
  • Builds a living knowledge base across investigations — entities, sources, tools, and methodology stay cross-linked in your vault. The AI remembers past work and applies it to new leads: "we already profiled this shell company in the Darfur case" instead of starting from zero.

What it integrates with

  • Agent runtimes — local journalism fine-tune, Claude Code, Gemini, Codex, OpenCode (multi-provider).
  • Web scraping, search, and evidence archiving — every source captured locally with chain of custody.
  • OSINT Navigator — 1,000+ curated OSINT tools with AI-powered lookup, surfaced live per task. Built by Indicator Media.
  • Knowledge vault ingestion (Obsidian) — investigations become permanent, searchable memory.
  • Monitoring feeds — conflict events, disaster alerts, global news, investigative outlets.
  • Browser automation, narrative tracking, misinformation databases — plug-in integrations.
