What it does

  • Takes a lead — URL, name, topic, document — and designs a methodology you approve before anything runs.
  • Runs up to 5 research cycles, each followed by an independent fact-checker with its own context.
  • Six readiness criteria must all pass before findings reach you.
  • Interactive HTML review — open in any browser, submit feedback, re-trigger the investigator.
  • Archives verified findings into your knowledge vault as structured, cross-linked knowledge.

What it integrates with

  • Agent runtimes — local journalism fine-tune, Claude Code, Gemini, Codex, OpenCode (multi-provider).
  • Web scraping, search, and evidence archiving — every source captured locally with chain of custody.
  • Tool-discovery API with 1,000+ curated OSINT tools, surfaced per task.
  • Knowledge vault ingestion (Obsidian) — investigations become permanent, searchable memory.
  • Monitoring feeds — conflict events, disaster alerts, global news, investigative outlets.
  • Browser automation, narrative tracking, misinformation databases — plug-in integrations.
